Our Story

Plattera was born from a simple observation — talented crochet artisans were creating beautiful handmade pieces, yet selling through unstructured channels like social media messages and informal orders.

Despite their creativity and dedication, many artisans struggled with:

  • Limited digital visibility
  • Manual order management
  • Payment confusion
  • Lack of professional storefronts
  • No structured review systems
"We believed crochet artisans deserved more. Plattera was created to provide a structured, transparent, and professional digital ecosystem exclusively for crochet creators."

Why a Crochet-Only Marketplace?

Crochet is not mass production.

Plattera is not a general marketplace — it is a curated crochet community.
